About

Writlet is the online application for letter writing!

Why

We at Writlet believe that the current forms of social media are too fast (instant),
this makes messages lose their value.

Therefore we created this application. An online writing simulator without the hassles of actually writing,
but with the benefits!
This writing simulator has a built-in delay for sending letters. Once you have sent your letter,
it will take some time before the letter arrives.

How

Register an account,
Find your penpals,
Write and receive letters!
